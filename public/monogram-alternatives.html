Monogram Alternatives

These four options are normalized to the same bounding box and preview scale so symmetry can be judged fairly. Each version is tuned for non-lopsided optical weight in header lockups and favicon reduction.

Current Active

Reference

The current production mark. Strong simplicity, but lighter right-side terminal can read slightly open at small sizes.

Alt 01 Balanced Line

Monoline

Most direct refinement. Equalized stem zones and calmer proportions while preserving the existing rhythm.

Alt 02 Framed Seal

Institutional

Adds a formal frame and bilateral spacing anchors. Most stable for governance-facing documents and headers.

Alt 03 Slab Cut

Structural

Heavier geometric mass with explicit center delimiter. High legibility under compression and stronger icon behavior.
