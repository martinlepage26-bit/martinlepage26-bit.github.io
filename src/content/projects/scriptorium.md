**Build the packet before the prose hardens.**

*SCRIPTO* is the public packet-builder surface. It turns a topic, archive, or governance problem into a bounded writing packet grounded in artifact classes, evidence hierarchy, recursive tests, and interface-bound controls.

The public app name is *SCRIPTO*. *Scriptorium* remains the longer method line behind it. The builder stays distinct from its companion tools: *Dr.Sort* handles corpus triage, *LOTUS* handles agency scoring, and *ECHO* handles listening through drafts.

The web surface offers a deterministic recursive builder: bounded, reviewable, and honest about what it does. Deeper local-first execution is being adapted from an MIT-licensed AutoResearchClaw base and reworked into Martin Lepage's own Scriptorium framing and method logic.
