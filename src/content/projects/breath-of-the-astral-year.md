*Breath of the Astral Year* is a self-published book by Martin Lepage available on Amazon. It treats gaialogy as a grammar of collective life, keeping signs, seasons, and social experience in the same frame.

The subtitle, *Gaialogy as a grammar of collective life*, keeps the focus on signs, seasons, and social life without collapsing the book into a technical manual or a proof-heavy gaialogy system.

The companion pages on this site now extend that public language in two directions: a sign glossary that makes the symbolic lexicon explicit, and an Astral app that turns the same vocabulary into a readable seasonal experience. They should be read as part of the published title&apos;s web presence rather than as evidence that the book is still only a manuscript.
