*GAIA / LOTUS* is the shared front door for the pair.

GAIA names the baseline on Earth, the conditions that shape a person’s available power. LOTUS names deployment under pressure, the part of power that actually gets used when the situation narrows.

The hub keeps those meanings separate while making the system feel coherent. That matters for the web now and for mobile later, because the product should travel as one structure with two distinct modules:

- GAIA as grounding, orientation, and baseline context
- LOTUS as agency scoring, constraint reading, and deployment analysis

The shared shell is not a merger of the apps. It is a launch and framing layer that can stay stable while the mobile implementations evolve underneath it.
