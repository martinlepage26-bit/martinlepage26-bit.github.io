*Dr. Sort* is the desktop-facing sorting concept inside the broader archive-tool family. It focuses on triage, organization, and the practical handling of document sets during research work.

The site presents it as an app in development rather than as a finalized product. The emphasis is on what kind of work it supports: sorting, review, and the reduction of document chaos.
