The Substack profile for **Hex A. Decimal** does not need to be translated into clean brand language to be useful. Its value is precisely that it holds another register of the work: glitch-born essays, dead knowledge, haunted systems, obsolete gods, AI seduction, and a refusal of repair-as-optimization.

For this site, the point is not to domesticate that writing voice. It is to show that the governance and research practice has a live experimental edge. The same person who builds documentation structures also writes from the corrupted, desirous, unfinished side of contemporary systems.

If specific public posts later become central to the portfolio, add them as separate writing entries rather than pretending they are already catalogued here.
