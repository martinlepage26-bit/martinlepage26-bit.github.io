Framing / About

Governance analysis as a relation between language, institution, system behavior, and consequence.

Work in this mode treats categories as operational instruments rather than neutral labels. Terms like risk, compliance, and safety are tested against evidence pathways, ownership structures, and decision effects.

Method 01

Method 04
