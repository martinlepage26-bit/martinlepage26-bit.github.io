Framing / About

Institutions stabilize language to stabilize authority. Governance starts by testing that stabilization.

The site voice is intentionally severe: claims are constrained, concepts are defined, and uncertainty is named directly. Editorial form is used as a governance instrument rather than a style layer.

When terms become untouchable, decisions become unaccountable.
